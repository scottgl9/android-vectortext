Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 25, 2025

Our Commitment: Vertext is designed with privacy as a core principle. All data processing happens entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information to external servers.

1. Introduction

Vertext ("we," "our," or "the app") is an SMS/MMS messaging application developed by Vanespark. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

By using Vertext, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Vertext is built to be completely private. We do not:

  • Collect or transmit your messages to any external server
  • Track your usage or behavior within the app
  • Collect analytics or telemetry data
  • Store any of your data on our servers
  • Share any information with third parties
  • Use advertising SDKs or tracking technologies
  • Access your data for any purpose beyond the core functionality of the app

3. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Vertext stores the following information locally on your device to provide its functionality:

3.1 Messages

Your SMS and MMS messages are accessed through Android's standard messaging provider. Vertext stores message metadata and content in a local database on your device for features like:

  • Displaying your conversations
  • Semantic search functionality
  • Message organization and categorization

3.2 Vector Embeddings

To enable semantic search, Vertext generates mathematical representations (embeddings) of your messages. These embeddings:

  • Are generated entirely on your device
  • Are stored only in the local database on your device
  • Are never transmitted to any external server
  • Cannot be reverse-engineered to recreate the original messages

3.3 Contacts

Vertext accesses your device's contacts to display contact names and photos in conversations. This data is never copied or stored separately from Android's contact provider.

3.4 App Preferences

Your app settings and preferences are stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage mechanisms.

4. Permissions

Vertext requires the following permissions to function:

  • SMS/MMS: To send, receive, and display text messages
  • Contacts: To show contact names and photos in conversations
  • Storage: To send and receive media attachments (photos, videos)
  • Notifications: To alert you of new messages
  • Phone State: For multi-SIM support and call integration

All permissions are used solely for the stated purposes and data accessed through these permissions never leaves your device.

5. AI and Machine Learning Features

Vertext includes AI-powered features such as semantic search and message summaries. All AI processing:

  • Runs entirely on your device
  • Uses on-device machine learning models
  • Does not require an internet connection
  • Never sends your data to external AI services

6. Data Security

We implement the following security measures to protect your data:

  • Local database encryption
  • Optional app lock with biometrics or PIN
  • Secure storage for sensitive preferences
  • No network transmission of personal data

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Data is retained as long as you keep the app installed
  • Uninstalling the app removes all app data from your device
  • You can clear app data at any time through Android settings
  • Message deletion in the app removes messages from local storage

8. Third-Party Services

Vertext does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app functions entirely offline for all core features.

9. Children's Privacy

Vertext is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since Vertext does not collect any personal information from any users, there is no risk of children's data being collected.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information through the app, please contact us. However, since all data remains on-device and is never transmitted to us, we have no access to any user data.

10. International Users

Vertext can be used anywhere in the world. Since no data is transmitted to external servers, there are no concerns about international data transfers. All your data remains on your device regardless of your location.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top
  • Providing notice through the app for significant changes

12. Your Rights

Since Vertext does not collect or store your personal data on any servers, traditional data subject rights (access, correction, deletion, portability) are inherently fulfilled:

  • Access: All your data is on your device and accessible to you
  • Correction: You can edit your data directly on your device
  • Deletion: You can delete data through the app or by uninstalling
  • Portability: You can use Android's backup features

13. Open Source

Vertext is open source software. You can review our source code to verify our privacy practices at our GitHub repository.

14. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@vanespark.com
  • Website: https://vanespark.com

Summary: Vertext is a privacy-first messaging app. We don't collect your data. We don't track you. We don't sell anything. Your messages stay on your device, period.
